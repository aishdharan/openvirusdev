Monthly variation of environmental factors and the prevalence of epidemic keratoconjunctivitis (EKC) in the district of Hyderabad. The month-wise prevalence of EKC was negatively correlated with the distribution of rainfall (a, r = -0.6894), humidity (b, r = -0.8111), and wind speed (d, r = -0.7535) but not with temperature (c, r = 0.2793)
