CT images of confirmed COVID-19-infected patients. ( A) A 37-year-old man with a 3-day history of fever and cough. The chest CT image showed GGOs with peripheral distribution in the lung. ( B) Local amplification of the GGOs. ( C) A 70-year-old man with a history of type 2 diabetes mellitus and hypertension. On the seventh day after coming back from Wuhan, he had fever and cough. Reticular interlobular septal thickening and typical crazy-paving pattern were shown by chest CT images. ( D) Local amplification of crazy-paving pattern.
