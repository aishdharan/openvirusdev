A 37-year-old man with a 3-day history of fever and cough. He had a history of close contact with COVID-19 confirmed cases and later he was confirmed to be COVID-19-infected by RT-PCR. ( A) The first chest CT acquired on February 12, 2020, showed small GGOs in the bilateral lungs. ( B) Eight days later, follow-up chest CT showed the GGOs developed rapidly. The size and range of the lesions increased, which indicated the deterioration of the disease.
