Infection, dissemination, transmission (A) and viral loads in abdomen, head/thorax and saliva (B-D) in Aedes albopictus La Réunion, 14 days after infection with CHIKV, DENV and ZIKV. Mosquitoes were infected with a blood meal at a titer of 10 7 ffu/mL and at 14 days post-infection, mosquitoes were processed to estimate the viral load in abdomen, head/thorax and saliva by titration on cells. Means are represented by horizontal bars. ns, non-significant, **p ≤ 0.01, Kruskal-Wallis test.
