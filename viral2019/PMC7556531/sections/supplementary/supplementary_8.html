S1 Fig

Dissemination and transmission efficiencies at different days (3, 7, 10, 14 and 21) after infection of Aedes albopictus Tunisia with CHIKV (A), DENV (B) and ZIKV (C). Dissemination efficiency refers to the proportion of mosquitoes with virus detected in head/thorax among the total number of mosquitoes examined, and transmission efficiency to the proportion of mosquitoes with virus detected in saliva among all mosquitoes examined.
