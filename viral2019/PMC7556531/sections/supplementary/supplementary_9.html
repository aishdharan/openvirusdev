Viral loads in abdomen (A) and head/thorax (B) after infection of Ae. albopictus Tunisia with CHIKV, DENV, and ZIKV. Mosquitoes were infected with a blood meal at a titer of 10 7 ffu/mL and were processed to estimate the viral load in abdomen and head/thorax by titration on cells. Means are represented by horizontal bars. (PDF)
