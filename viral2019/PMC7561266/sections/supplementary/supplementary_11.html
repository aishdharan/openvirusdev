Location of BG-traps in Madeira Island. The distribution of BG-traps location across the island, as reported in the Entomological panel bulletins (PEnt_RAMW52/2019, in Portuguese) of the Institute of Health Administration, IP-RAM (IASAÚDE IP-RAM). The colored dots on the map, represents the cumulative number of adult mosquitoes in each location for the week 10, 2018 to week 52, 2019. Blue = 0%, Dark green = 1%-10%, Light green = 11%–30%, Yellow = 31%–50%, Orange = 51%–60%, Red = 61%–100%. Cumulative number of adult mosquitoes for all location = 3464. Adapted from Institute of Health Administration, IP-RAM [ 34]. (TIF)
